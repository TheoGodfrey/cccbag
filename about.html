Who we are

Chadlington Climate Change and Biodiversity Action Group is a community-led initiative dedicated to safeguarding our natural environment for future generations. Our mission is simple yet vital: to foster nature recovery, protect local biodiversity, and map the natural assets that make our area unique.

We believe that local action can drive global change. By working together as neighbours and stewards of our environment, we strive to create a sustainable future while celebrating the rich ecological heritage of our parish.

Through hands-on projects, educational events, and collaborative efforts, we are committed to:

  • Restoring habitats and enhancing biodiversity across the parish.
  • Engaging the community in identifying and preserving our natural treasures.
  • Promoting sustainable practices to mitigate the impacts of climate change.

Whether you're passionate about wildlife, curious about conservation, or simply looking to make a difference, there's a place for you in our growing movement. Join us as we work towards a greener, more vibrant Chadlington—one small step at a time.

Together, let's nurture nature and inspire positive change.
