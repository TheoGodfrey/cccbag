Our Projects

We record, study and share what we find in and around Chadlington — from the insects in our meadows to the quality of the water in the Evenlode. Each project is community-led and open to all.

Butterflies & Moths

We have recorded 24 butterfly species in and around Chadlington, along with a growing list of moths from light-trap nights. Find the full species list, collages, and links to further reading here.

Evenlode Water Quality

The River Evenlode runs through our parish and is under increasing pressure from agricultural runoff and sewage. We are tracking water quality data and working with local partners to improve it.
