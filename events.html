Evenlode Geographical Lecture — Autumn 2025

Wednesday 8 October 2025  ·  19:00 – 20:30
Chadlington Memorial Hall, OX7 3LZ

Local speakers sharing local and global ideas for tomorrow. Three talks covering Chadlington's history (Oxfordshire VCH), tree-living honey bees, and forest peoples' rights from Chadlington to Borneo. Tea and cake from 6.15pm; pay-bar 8.30–9.30pm by Chadlington Brewery. Tickets £10 (Students £5) from Café de la Post (01608 676461).

Evenlode Geographical Lectures at the Memorial Hall, Chadlington OX7 3LZ. 7.00–8.30pm (Tea and Cake from 6.15pm). 80 tickets on sale at Café de la Post £10 (Students £5) from 2nd April 2025, or call 01608 676 461 and speak to Tracy Waldron BEM. All funds go to the Chadlington Memorial Hall.

Chadlington CC & BioD — Third Tite Event

Wednesday 20 November 2024  ·  18:00 – 19:00
Tite Inn, Chadlington

Two keynote speakers on Chadlington's biodiversity interests, plus updates on the Big Butterfly Count and Parish Land App. We also announced the Householder Energy Event for April 2025. As usual we were in the 'tent' outside — attendees were advised to bring a coat!
